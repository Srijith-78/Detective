The case starts with a list of murders in Chennai city. Firstly, there's a dead body chopped into pieces and mixed with beef biriyani and served in a hotel, only to horrify the customer, when she finds a human finger. Next, a middle aged women is killed and put inside the water tank of a company, but her palms are stuck in the notice board. Then, the head of a middle aged lady is found inside a wooden box hung in the traffic signal. You are Detective Arjun, and you, with your assistant, Kajal, are consulted by the Assistant Commissioner of Chennai City, Mr. Aravind. A day ago, a small boy named Dinesh reports to them, his neighbor that his mother is missing. Go ahead and question the clues to find the killer.
